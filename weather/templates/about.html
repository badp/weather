About this site

The usual approach to polling is to poll infrequently and ask a lot of questions. I want to see what happens when you ask one question but ask it often.

The one question: “How’s it going?”

That is all. Please let us know about your general feelings. Had a great day? Had a rough morning? Stomach upset? How do you feel about the direction of your team? Is that deadline filling you with dread and gloom? Do you feel a black hole in your stomach as you check your email?

Don't think too hard about it. Just pick the emoticon that matches your overall mood more closely.

If you feel the need to express yourself more fully, simply talk to your manager or your fellow coworkers.
